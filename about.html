🌿 Benefits of Growing Plants

Improves Air Quality – Plants absorb carbon dioxide and release oxygen, making the environment fresher and healthier.

  1. Reduces Stress – Taking care of plants helps calm the mind and reduce anxiety.
  2. Boosts Productivity – Green surroundings increase focus, creativity, and energy levels.
  3. Supports the Environment – Plants help fight climate change by reducing pollution and improving soil quality.
  4. Brings Positivity – A green space creates a peaceful and positive atmosphere.
  5. Encourages Responsibility – Caring for plants teaches consistency, responsibility, and patience.
  6. Promotes Sustainability – Growing your own plants or food reduces waste and supports eco-friendly living.

🌱 How GrowPlants2Earn Works

GrowPlants2Earn is a decentralized application (dApp) that rewards users for contributing to a greener world.

  • Take a photo while you are growing your plants to document progress.
  • Submit the photo through the dApp for verification.
  • Receive B3TR rewards based on activity and the number of plants you grow.

Capture more growth — earn more rewards. The more plants you grow, the more B3TR you earn.
